Seattle Link 3D

A real-time 3D map of Sound Transit's Link light rail — the 1 Line (Lynnwood → Seattle → SeaTac → Federal Way) and the 2 Line (Seattle → Bellevue → Redmond).

Positions come live from the OneBusAway Puget Sound API. When live data is unavailable, the app runs a realistic schedule simulation (see the badge in the top-middle).

Interaction

  • Click a train — details + follow it with a chase camera
  • Click a station — live arrivals board
  • Drag to pan · right-drag / two fingers to rotate & tilt · scroll to zoom

Keyboard

  • F follow selected train
  • N jump to next train
  • C cycle follow camera
  • D day / night
  • H reset view
  • B buildings
  • Esc close / stop following
